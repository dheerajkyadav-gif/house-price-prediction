A TYCS student at Universal College created this House Price Prediction Website as my Final Year Project to help people get a sense of the prices of houses in the City of Mumbai.

As a TYCS student at Universal College, I've developed a user-friendly website dedicated to simplifying the process of predicting house prices. Combining my passion for technology with a desire to make a positive impact, I meticulously designed algorithms to analyze various factors and provide accurate predictions. The interface is intuitive and accessible, catering to individuals of all backgrounds. Each line of code was purposefully written to alleviate the stress and uncertainty often associated with real estate decisions. Through perseverance, I've created a platform that empowers users to confidently navigate the housing market. Witnessing the positive impact of my website reaffirms the significance of my endeavor, highlighting the transformative power of technology when wielded with purpose and empathy.
